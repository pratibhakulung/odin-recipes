Quick Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smoothie

Description

This is a fast and filling smoothie with a deep-pink color and a rich, creamy texture.

Ingridents
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds
  • 14 frozen strawberries
  • 6 ounces nonfat vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1 banana, broken into chunks
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
Steps
  1. Blend oats and chia seeds together in a blender to a fine consistency.
  2. Add strawberries, yogurt, banana, almond milk, and vanilla extract
  3. blend until smooth.
